Where It All Began

Life, unlike anywhere in the universe, had its beginnings on the mysterious planet of Eggtopia. The enigmatic age-old question of which came first, the egg or the chic was answered here on this planet. As the tale goes, the first ChicKing and ChicQueen emerged from one mysterious "EGG."

However, destiny had other plans…

The Great Meteorite

A meteorite with otherworldly light crashed down in the center of Eggtopia, sending shockwaves across the entirety of the planet. There was something more than just an ordinary event. The impact of the meteorite changed the very fabric of life on Eggtopia and, in turn, affected every chic that walked on its surface.

Once ordinary creatures, the chics found themselves transformed. Their outward forms now seemed to reflect their innermost natures. The extraordinary abilities brought forth changed from simple beings into powerful entities, each different in its new abilities.

CHICKKINGS WARRIOR HIERARCHY

With the occurrence of the meteorite event, the chics naturally formed into three leagues, each unique and individual. They were no longer bound by their genders; possessing a newfound power and purpose:

IMMORTALS

The most powerful defense of Eggtopia—no strength surpassed, no resolve broken. The Immortals were the first line of defense for the planet, determined to fight the threats to their home at all costs. Referred to in tones rarely above a whisper, they were revered and held in fear.

MAJESTIC

The graceful and wise Majestics found their place in the balancing act as peacekeepers. They had the skill to talk one down or mediate between the inhabitants of Eggtopia to keep peace among them. Despite the tumult around, peace was a prevailing force in this surreal realm of chics.

NOBLE

Though not as powerful as the Immortals nor as influential as the Majestics, they were the heart and soul of Eggtopia. They were the civilians, toiling tirelessly with their might and minds to keep the planet alive and flourishing. The Nobles were the nurturers of the land and built their thriving communities.

A New Threat

For many cycles, the balance of power among the leagues spelled a peaceful and prosperous haven for Eggtopia. Now a new threat has come knocking on its door. A formidable foe from the darkest corners of the universe set its eyes on Eggtopia. The source of this sinister curse was the "Obsidian Egg."


The Obsidian Egg, with its malevolent aura, had been cast upon Eggtopia disrupting the ecosystem and slowly poisoning the very essence of the planet. This malevolent force seemed determined to destroy Eggtopia from within, its dark energy seeping into every corner of the planet.

COMING SOON

Creation of the Fighter Cocks

The Eggtopians knew that they had to find a way to break the curse and restore their world to its former glory. For the first time in chic history, the three leagues set aside their differences, coming together for the greater good. And out of this union arose “ChicEgg,” from which a new race of brave warriors, “the Fighter Cocks, came into existence.”

The Fighter Cocks had the raw power of the Immortals, the wisdom of the Majestics, and the resilience of the Nobles. They were the ultimate defenders, the ones that could combat any emerging threat for the sake of their home planet.

With 5,000 ChicKing and 5,000 ChicQueen, Eggtopia had a new army of Fighter Cocks. The training was grueling, with intense battles ahead, but the resolve of the Fighter Cocks was ironclad. Every single one of them knew that the fate of Eggtopia hung in the balance.

An epic clash of forces was about to take place. The journey to save Eggtopia had begun, and its fate now rested in the hands of those brave enough to confront the Obsidian Egg and unravel the secrets that bound it.
